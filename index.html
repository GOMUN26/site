Official Committees

Rigorous debate and current global topics designed to challenge your negotiation and public speaking skills.

General Assembly (GA)

The General Assembly is particularly important when issues involve sovereignty, non-intervention, and respect for international law, as it represents the collective voice of the international community.

Background Paper
Chair: Ivanna Carmona
 Moderator: Franco Huerta Delon

Security Council (SC)

It gives primary responsibility for maintaining international peace and security to the Security Council, which may meet whenever peace is threatened.

Background Paper
Chair: GREGORIO BARRADAS TRES
 Moderator: OSCAR FERNANDEZ BEIRANA

United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)

ECOSOC serves as the central forum for discussing international economic and social issues, and formulating policy recommendations addressed to member states and the United Nations System.

Background Paper
Chair:
Moderator: Pia Zambrano Mora

UNICEF

UNICEF (United Nations International Children's Emergency Fund), is an agency of the United Nations responsible for providing humanitarian and developmental aid to children worldwide.

Background Paper
Chair: EMILIANO SALAZAR
Moderator: VALENTINA PEREZ

Commission on the Status of Women (CSW)

The Commission on the Status of Women (CSW) is the principal global intergovernmental body exclusively dedicated to the promotion of gender equality, the rights and the empowerment of women.

Background Paper
Chair: ARTURO GONZALEZ MENESES
 Moderator: ALDARA RODRIGUEZ DE LA FUENTE

INTERPOL

Interpol provides investigative support, expertise and training to law enforcement worldwide, focusing on three major areas of transnational crime: terrorism, cybercrime and organized crime.

Background Paper
Chair: OMAR MARTINEZ
 Moderator: MATHIAS PADRON

Federación Internacional de Fútbol Asociación

La FIFA regula y promueve el fútbol global en todos los niveles, desde las bases hasta la élite. Su labor consiste en fijar las reglas del juego e impulsar este deporte mediante la innovación, la educación y el trabajo conjunto con sus 211 federaciones miembro.

Background Paper
Presidente:Rafael Arturo Astudillo Castillo
 Moderadora: Mayte Lopez Vichi

UN Fashion

Recognizing the fashion and lifestyle industries' profound environmental, economic, and societal influence, the UN Fashion and Lifestyle Network actively advocates for transparent, inclusive, and transformative engagement among global stakeholders.

Background Paper
Chair: Sofia Aroche
 Moderator: Ximena Ruiz

Conferencia Nacional de Gobernadores (CONAGO)

Creada en 2002, la Conferencia Nacional de Gobernadores es un espacio de diálogo y coordinación entre los estados de México para fortalecer el federalismo y la cooperación con el gobierno federal.

Background Paper
Presidente: ELVIRA QUESADA RODRIGUEZ
 Moderador: JUAN PABLO MARTINEZ

European Parliament

The European Parliament serves as the directly elected legislative body of the European Union, representing the interests of citizens across EU member states.

Background Paper
Chair:
Moderator: LUCA IGLESIAS

International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA)

The International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) is an intergovernmental organization that seeks to promote the peaceful use of nuclear energy and to inhibit its use for any military purpose, including nuclear weapons.

Background Paper
Chair:
 Moderator:

World Food Programme (WFP)

The World Food Programme is an international organization within the United Nations that provides food assistance worldwide. It is the world's largest humanitarian organization and the leading provider of school meals.

Background Paper
Chair: MARIA JULIA MARTINEZ
 Moderator: DOMINIKA DAGUER DÍAZ
